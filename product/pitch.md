# Turbo Notify Pitch

> A micro-SaaS that simplifies sending messages via WhatsApp for systems and platforms.

---

## The Problem

Companies wanting to send messages on behalf of their customers face expensive and time-consuming processes to enable each number on WhatsApp Business. This delays validations and prevents automation of simple flows.

## The Solution

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Single API** | Send and receive messages through one endpoint |
| **On-demand Numbers** | WhatsApp numbers managed by Turbo Notify |
| **Fast Onboarding** | Activation in minutes, no Meta validation |
| **Pay-as-you-go** | Pay only for what you use |

## Competitive Advantages

- **Less bureaucracy**: No direct contact with Meta required
- **Predictable cost**: Plans by message volume and additional numbers
- **Developer focus**: SDKs and clear documentation
- **Scale ready**: Infrastructure for thousands of messages per minute

## Market Opportunity

WhatsApp dominates communication in Brazil and many other countries. Small platforms and integrators need a cheap and fast solution to validate their products and scale customer communication.

## Roadmap

1. Consolidate production sending infrastructure
2. Publish self-service dashboard
3. Expand integrations with CRMs and automation platforms
4. Launch reseller programs for partners

---

## Vision

Turbo Notify aims to make sending WhatsApp messages as simple as making an HTTP request, freeing businesses to focus on their product.
